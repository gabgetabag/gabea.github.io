Welcome to the Terminator Movie Fan Page

The Terminator Franchise,created by James Cameron, is a science-fiction action film series that is about a futuristic battle between humanity and advanced machines, with a focus on the iconic Terminator antagonist/protagonist (depending on which film)..

About Terminator

Terminator is a classic film series, renowned for its groundbreaking visual effects, (which were way ahead of its time), exciting action, and rememberable characters.

Key Aspects

  • Terminator 3: Rise of the Machines (2003)

  • Terminator Genisys (2015)
  • Terminator: Dark Fate (2019)
